# About Me

> Use this when: you need to introduce me, write a bio, or understand my background.

I'm Antoine Boxho. I'm a software engineer based in London, and the first hire at [Scope AI](https://scope.ai/), where we're automating one of the most overlooked industries in the world with language models.

## How I got here

I trained as a financial engineer at [Solvay Brussels School](https://www.solvay.edu/), with a year at the [National University of Singapore](https://nus.edu.sg/) where I picked up a minor in computer science. My master's thesis was on blockchain as financial infrastructure, which turned into a [published primer](https://dipot.ulb.ac.be/dspace/bitstream/2013/255509/3/MA2_Boxho.pdf) in 2017.

I started writing software professionally at [FinMetrics](https://www.finmetrics.com/) in Geneva, building calculation engines for portfolio valuation, value-at-risk, and stress testing for large European treasuries and private banks. Around the same time I co-founded [Takeo.io](https://takeo.io/), a productivity startup building a secure enterprise communication platform.

After that I joined [McKinsey & Company](https://www.mckinsey.com/) in Brussels in the Core Tech & Analytics practice. For close to four years I shipped ML-backed demand forecasting, clustering analytics, automation, and data pipelines for MedTech, Telco, and large enterprise clients. I also did a summer at McKinsey as a data analyst a few years earlier, before I'd fully committed to engineering.

In August 2024 I moved to London as the first hire at Scope AI. We fine-tune LLMs and document layout models on industry-specific data, build the distributed systems and pipelines that capture field data in real time, and ship a product aimed at taking manual toil out of an industry that nobody wants to touch.

## How I think about the work

I care about shipping. I prefer simple, obvious code over clever abstractions, small focused functions, and systems that a junior engineer can pick up and maintain. I reach for prototypes before specs. I tend to work across the stack — infra, pipelines, ML, frontend — and will happily go deep on whichever piece is on fire.

## Outside work

French and Dutch native, fully fluent in English. I play with code for fun, travel when I can, and keep a soft spot for financial markets from my earlier life.
